About Me

Hi, I'm Dally — I build the systems that run quietly behind a growing business.

I'm a GoHighLevel Specialist with 1.5 years of hands-on experience helping businesses streamline operations, automate processes, generate leads, and improve customer engagement through GoHighLevel. My work sits at the intersection of CRM strategy and day-to-day execution — setting up the backend, building the automations, and making sure the systems actually hold up once real leads start flowing through them.

I've supported multiple business operations end-to-end: from agency and sub-account configuration to client onboarding, workflow design, and reporting. I care about the details that most people skip — the dropped follow-up, the workflow that breaks on edge cases, the pipeline stage nobody's tracking.

Above all, I'm detail-oriented and process-driven, with a habit of approaching every account the same way: understand the business first, then design something scalable enough to grow with it.
